Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

At DOSE ("we," "our," "us"), we're committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

DOSE stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data.

1.1 Local Data Storage

Your wellness data is stored exclusively on your device using your device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage). This includes:

  • Daily DOSE completions
  • Journal entries ("How did that feel?" reflections)
  • Action selections
  • XP/level progression and streak counts
  • Chemical Mastery badge unlocks
  • Weekly Pulse check-in responses

1.2 What We Do NOT Collect

DOSE does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Account information or user accounts of any kind
  • Email addresses or personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics or usage data
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Device information or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contacts, calendar, or address book data
  • Health Kit, medical records, or biometric data
  • Microphone or camera access
  • Any other personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

All processing happens locally on your device. We have no servers and no access to your data. Your data is used only for the following core functionality:

  • Storing your daily DOSE actions and progress
  • Calculating streaks and XP
  • Enabling local notifications at your chosen time
  • Tracking your wellness journal entries and reflections

Your data never leaves your device. We do not use it for analytics, marketing, advertisements, or any form of profiling.

3. Third-Party Services

DOSE does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. There are no third-party integrations, cloud databases, analytics platforms, or crash reporting services.

The only third-party involvement is with the App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android) for purchasing and downloading the app. These platforms handle payment processing and app distribution according to their own privacy policies. See section 11 below for details.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using your operating system's built-in storage. We do not operate servers, databases, or any backend infrastructure.

  • Local Storage: Your wellness data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Device Encryption: Your device's built-in operating system encryption protects your data at rest
  • No Servers: DOSE has no servers, cloud infrastructure, or backend systems to compromise
  • No Access: We cannot access, view, or interact with your data in any way

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data exists only on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud backups, and no server copies.

  • Device Storage: Your wellness data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app
  • Deletion: Deleting the DOSE app from your device permanently removes all associated data
  • No Backups: We maintain no copies, backups, or archives of your data on any server or external service
  • Complete Privacy: Your data is completely under your control and is never shared with us or any third party

6.2 CCPA (California Residents)

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete your personal information

  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
